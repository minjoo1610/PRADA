  • User's Manual and Handling Precautions
    for clothing
    1. When washing clothes, please refer to the washing instructions on the care label attached to the inside of the product.
    Leather/textile bags, wallets and shoes
    1. Dry in a well-ventilated shade as it may deteriorate if it is dried directly in direct sunlight or heat when wet.
    2. When storing, avoid places with high temperature or humidity and keep the contents by inserting the contents inside.
    3. Avoid using oil or near fire.
    4. The endothelium of the shoe with natural leather color is the nature of the contact areas of the skin fold may eonal white or light-colored
      socks should preferably be avoided.
    5. In the case of a closed (stitch-down) design, water may permeate, so avoid wearing it in the rain as much as possible.
    6. Polyurethane windows are lightweight and have excellent shock absorption, but they are adhered by the press compression method, so the windows may fall off, but this is not a quality problem.
    7. Print technique and pearl pigment coating products are different from dye penetration dyeing technique, so they may fall off when used, but it is not a quality problem due to the nature of the process.
    8. Due to the nature of leather or fabric, repeated wear and tear may cause wrinkles and wear on the product.
    9. When other materials come into contact with the patent material, adsorption and color transfer may occur, but this is a unique characteristic of the material and has nothing to do with the durability of the product.
    for sunglasses
    1. If it gets dirty, wash it under running water and let it dry naturally.
    2. If oil, etc. gets on it, use a neutral detergent.
    3. Wipe off any water droplets on the lens surface using a soft cloth made of natural material.
    4. This product may be deformed if left under direct sunlight for a long time.
    for perfume
    1. Keep out of reach of children.
    2. Store away from direct sunlight.
    3. In case of contact with eyes or mucous membranes, immediately rinse with running water and consult a doctor.

    If you wish to return a product purchased from the E-Store, you can return it for free within 14 days from the date of delivery, and the returned product complies with the terms of purchase. You can request a return within 7 days of receiving the product, and you must return the product with a return number within 7 days after requesting a return. Before applying for a return, please check the return conditions below according to the terms and conditions of purchase .

    1. Products must be returned in the condition they were delivered in and in their original packaging. The customer must ensure that the product is undamaged, complete, has not diminished in value through use or consumption, and is in its original packaging, including all labels, undamaged tags and security tags, as in the condition at the time of shipment. It must be returned in good condition.
    2. If the product has a security tag attached, returns of this product are not accepted if the security tag has been removed, broken or damaged.
    3. Perfume must be unopened.
    4. All products must be returned through the shipping company Ilyang Logis.
    5. You can only return the product to the same address it was delivered to. Custom-made products that permanently remain on the product, such as hot stamping made upon customer request, cannot be returned.
