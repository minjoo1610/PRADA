PRADASPHERE PRADASPHERE

SS 2022 Womenswear FASHION SHOWS

  • Temptation through savings. For the Spring/Summer 2022 Prada collection, co-creative directors Miuccia Prada and Raf Simons came up with a way to seduce and express sexuality through clothing. The process of taking off clothes one by one inevitably emphasizes or exposes the body.

    Existing clothes have been explored and reinterpreted.

    The collection follows the concepts and contours of clothing, such as the train (the tail-like part of the back of a skirt or dress),

    most essential elements. Their structure is intrinsically linked to the body, and thus to temptation. Traces of the past always shape our present. The skeleton or lacing decoration of the stitched corset,
    like the shadow of clothes, creates a new item, and the corset releases the body rather than holding it, revealing the skin inside it.

    The train uses couture silk material. Each look has been reconstructed to negate the traditional meaning of restraint, which frees the body.

    The word elegance in evening wear applies to the entire collection,

    and materials such as double satin and scarlet lace are used in unexpected situations. By changing your perspective, you question these symbols and

    their meanings. The fantasy inherent in evening wear is balanced with everyday reality.

  • Simultaneous viewing exemplifies the modern world: we observe, interact,
    and communicate in many different ways. The Spring/Summer 2022 Prada Show

    will be held simultaneously at the Fondazione Prada Deposito in Milan and Bund 1 in Shanghai. Within that stage, video screens bring each show into dialogue with

    physical and virtual reality, bringing two groups of people from both ends of the world together in modern society.
