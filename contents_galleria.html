Galleria

  • Since its birth in 2007, the Galleria bag has been constantly reinterpreted as a neo-classic and quintessential Prada.

    The Galleria bag, which continues to be a legacy and representative bag of the future, derives its name from Prada's historic flagship store,

    Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II, located in Milan, where Miuccia Prada's grandfather,

    Mario Prada, founded the Prada brand in 1913. Established boutiques are located here.

  • This design, which symbolizes Prada's 21st century identity, is for the first time crafted from saffiano leather,

    a material that Mario Prada has applied for. It is the main material used repeatedly.

    Reminiscent of mid-20th century frame designs and Madison-style bags, the Galleria bag is an active take on the classics of the past and represents Prada's centuries of craftsmanship and excellence in leatherworking.

    Film director Xavier Dolan expresses a personal perspective on the identity of the Prada Galleria in a short video inspired by the relentless fascination with fashion icons.
